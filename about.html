Definition

Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans.

Types of AI

  • Narrow AI: Performs a specific task (e.g., voice recognition).
  • General AI: Understands and performs any intellectual task like a human.
  • Superintelligent AI: Surpasses human intelligence (theoretical).
